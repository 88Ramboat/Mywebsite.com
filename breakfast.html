Our Breakfast Experience

At Cozy B&B, we believe breakfast should be memorable. Each morning, our guests wake up to a variety of fresh, flavorful dishes made with care. Our ingredients are locally sourced whenever possible, ensuring seasonal freshness and quality.

Fresh Baked Goods

  • Buttermilk Pancakes: Light, fluffy, and served with pure Vermont maple syrup and a pat of creamy butter. Add seasonal berries for an extra touch of sweetness.
  • Farmhouse French Toast: Thick-sliced bread soaked in an egg-vanilla batter, topped with powdered sugar and a side of our spiced apple compote.
  • Blueberry Scones: Buttery, crumbly scones bursting with blueberries and finished with a lemon glaze. Perfect with a cup of hot tea.

Egg Dishes & Hearty Options

  • Garden Omelet: Three farm-fresh eggs folded around sautéed spinach, mushrooms, and goat cheese. Served with a side of roasted potatoes.
  • Country Scramble: Cage-free eggs scrambled with bell peppers, onions, and cheddar cheese. Comes with a side of house-made sausage links.
  • Herb & Vegetable Frittata: An open-faced Italian-style omelet infused with fresh basil, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and feta cheese.

Light & Refreshing Selections

  • Greek Yogurt Parfait: Creamy yogurt layered with house-made granola and honey-sweetened berries. A light, energizing start to your day.
  • Seasonal Fruit Plate: A selection of ripe, hand-cut fruits such as melons, berries, pineapple, and grapes, arranged to look as good as they taste.
  • Avocado Toast: Whole-grain toast topped with smashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. Add a poached egg for extra protein.

Beverage Options

  • Freshly Brewed Coffee: Sourced from a local roaster, our medium-roast blend is smooth and aromatic. Served hot, with cream and sugar on request.
  • Selection of Teas: Black, green, and herbal teas sourced from premium estates. Enjoy with honey or lemon for a soothing cup.
  • Juices & More: Orange, cranberry, and apple juices are always available, along with sparkling water or milk.

We’re happy to accommodate dietary preferences—just let us know if you’re vegan, gluten-free, or have any other special requests. Our goal is to start your day on a delicious note, leaving you energized for everything that awaits during your stay at Cozy B&B.
