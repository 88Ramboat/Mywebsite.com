Your Personal Retreat

Each of our rooms is designed to offer restful sleep and a peaceful atmosphere. Soft linens, plush pillows, and gentle lighting create a soothing environment after a day of exploring. Enjoy a quiet evening with a book, wake up to birdsong, and start your morning refreshed and ready for a hearty breakfast.

Garden Room

The Garden Room features a cozy queen bed, windows overlooking our flower gardens, and a private bath. Open the curtains to let in natural light and enjoy scents of fresh blooms drifting in on a gentle breeze.

Sunrise Suite

Our most spacious accommodation, the Sunrise Suite boasts a king bed, a comfortable seating area, and a small private balcony perfect for greeting the day with a cup of coffee. Warm hues and soft textures create an inviting, luxurious retreat.

Bluebird Room

The Bluebird Room offers two twin beds and cheerful décor inspired by the birds you’ll spot at our feeders. Sunlight filters through lacy curtains, and a desk by the window invites you to journal, sketch, or simply reflect on the day’s adventures.

No matter which room you choose, you’ll find thoughtful touches—soft robes, ample storage, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a small welcome treat. Our goal is for you to feel truly at ease, surrounded by comfort and the quiet hum of countryside life.
